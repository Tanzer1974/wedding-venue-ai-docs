3. AI Image Generation

Our app uses third-party AI services to generate wedding designs. Here's how it works:

  1. Your photo is sent securely to our AI processing partner
  2. The AI generates your design based on your selections
  3. Your original photo is processed and stored only as needed
  4. Generated designs are saved to your account for your design history
Important: We do not use your photos to train AI models. Your images are used solely to generate your requested designs.
